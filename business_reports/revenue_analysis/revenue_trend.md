## 📊 Revenue Trend Analysis

![Revenue Trend]((../insights_images/revenue_trend.png))

### Key Insights

* Revenue shows a **clear declining long-term trend** (running average decreasing steadily).

* Monthly performance is **highly volatile**, with sharp fluctuations across periods.

* A **significant drop occurs in early 2023**, indicating a structural shift in performance.

* The decline is **sustained (not temporary)**, as confirmed by the rolling average.

* Late 2023 shows **partial recovery**, but revenue remains below earlier peaks.

### Business Interpretation

* The declining trend suggests **underlying weaknesses in revenue generation**, not just short-term variation.

* High volatility indicates **inconsistent demand**, impacting predictability and planning.

* The early 2023 drop is a **critical inflection point**, requiring deeper investigation.

* Weak recovery suggests **current strategies are insufficient to restore growth**.
